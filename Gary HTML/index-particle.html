About Me

I am currently completing an MSc in Management Consultancy at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School. Previously, I completed a BSc in Business Information Systems at University College Cork.

My journey to date has been driven by my interest in technology subjects, human relations and business. At UCC, I chose a course that gave me many opportunities to learn and develop key technical skills that I could then apply to real-world problems. For instance, for my final year project, I developed a mobile health app to improve the quality of Dementia patients lives. During my internships at Apple and 100MileMakers, I deepened my understanding of business and appreciated how technology can be applied to achieve business goals. At Smurfit, I enjoy the practical elements of the course, working with clients and applying my problem solving and analytical skills in presenting solutions for clients. Furthermore, I took advantage of extracurricular activities to work in teams and develop my interpersonal skills. The Irish Student Consulting Group elected me to be the project lead for a team of six consultants on a research project for a UK based client.

I am committed to a commercial career and convinced that technology consulting will best allow me to apply my problem solving skills to complex business problems and work in teams.
